Frequently asked questions

What is forUS?

forUS is a website that allows thier users to sell the clothes they don't want anymore. This website allows users to make extra money for the clothes they don't want or wear anymore. We have created this website to help women who suffer from breast cancer and we expect to spread awareness. 40% of our profits go to the breast cancer research foundation.

What can I sell in forUS?

We are mainly focused on women clothing. You can sell various items such as:

  • T-shirt
  • Tops
  • Jeans
  • Scarf
  • Jackets
  • Coats
  • Skirt
  • Shorts
  • Jumpers
  • Joggers
  • Heels
  • Accessories
  • Leggings

    • How do I change my username?

    Once you've created your account, you won't be able to change your username. This is just to reduce the confusion about the sellers for the buyers.

    What payment methods can I use?

    Buyers of forUS are able to use major debits and credits cards. We also accept payments through apple pay and google pay.

    Can I sell without creating an account?

    In simple words, the answer would be NO! The reason we don't allow customers to sell without having a account is because we need to make sure we have enough details about the buyer so they can be trusted.

    How long does it take to receive my item?

    Your item should be with you in the 3-5 working days from the day you placed your order.

    Can my item be exchanged or refunded?

    Unfortunately for our forUS customers, we don't have a return or exchange policy.

    How can I track my order?

    To track your order, you'll need to have a tracking number sent to you by the seller once your order has been placed. Once you've received your tracking nuumber, you would need to go to the delivery company's website and paste in your tracking Id there.
